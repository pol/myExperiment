About:

The myExperiment Virtual Research Environment enables you and your colleagues to share digital items associated with your research &mdash in particular it enables you to share and execute scientific workflows.

You can use myexperiment.org to find publicly available workflows. If you want further access, and the ability to upload and share workflows, you will need to sign up.

The software that powers myexperiment.org is downloadable so that you can run your own myExperiment instance.

See the myExperiment Wiki for further information about myExperiment and how to join the user and developer communities.
